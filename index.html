What is Crossfit?

Crossfit is a program that concentrates on varied functional movement preformed at high intensity to achieve physical fitness.

Crossfit is a combination of gymanstic, weight-lifting, throwing, running and rowing movements. It creates neurological and physical fitness, increasing balance, strength, flexibility, agility, accuracy, and endurance.

Why Crossfit?

  1. It's fun!
  2. You'll get physically fit, faster
  3. You'll love the new changes to your body and health
  4. You'll gain a new social network
