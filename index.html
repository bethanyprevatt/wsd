What is Crossfit?

Crossfit is a program that concentrates on varied functional movement preformed at high intensity to achieve physical fitness. Crossfit will then target major components of physical fitness. Crossfit is a combination of gymanstic, weight-lifting, throwing, running and rowing movements. It creates neurological and physical fitness, increasing balance, strength, flexibility, agility, accuracy, and endurance. Crossfit workout training way requires you to workout 3-5 days with the high intensity workouts that usually take from 5-15 minutes to complete.

Why Crossfit?

  1. It's fun!
  2. You'll get physically fit, faster
  3. You'll love the new changes to your body and health
  4. You'll gain a new social network
